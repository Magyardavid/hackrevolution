About Us

Who are we?

Our StartUp is about solving problems and optimizing situations that arise in the CraftHack Challenge. For example, the outdated entry system, difficult navigation, combining many platforms used by competitors, etc.

We are working to make it easy for riders to enjoy this 24-hour challenge without any hassle or problems, and really only have to face the difficulties of the Challenge.

Services

Authentication

Reworked authentication system

Our idea would revolutionize the participant experience for a more pleasant and enjoyable time for everyone. We would change the way of authentication, by revamping the physical cards and making them digital with Google and Apple wallet cards and taking ticket control onto staff’s mobile devices.

Validation

Brainstorming / Idea validator AI

Also, we would help teams come up with ideas, or help them break out from tunnel vision by an AI idea validator, that would review their ideas and point out the weaknesses and place to improve on.

Tem Making

AI Team making possibilities

Another part of our app would be a team maker for those, who don’t have a team, based on their knowledge they would be put in teams where their knowledge can be used for the best!

Unity

Unified platform

Also, we found it infuriating that we have to use many platforms throughout hackathons, in order to face this problem, we would merge communication, submission, and jurying into one unified platform.

Navigation

Venue navigation

Our app has an online map with geolocation that helps you navigate to your destination. It provides accurate information about your location and detailed directions. You can easily explore the whole hackaton’s place and you can make your route plan fastly.
